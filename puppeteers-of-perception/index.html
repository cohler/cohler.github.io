How Artificial Intelligence (AI) Systems are Designed to Mislead

Jonathan Cohler
Cohler & Associates, Inc. • Lexington, MA, USA • cohler@post.harvard.edu
Fall 2024
Abstract

Artificial intelligence is playing an increasingly prominent role in medicine, warfare, and virtually all aspects of the economy. It is an amazingly powerful tool, and all need to be acquainted with its potential as well as its dangers.
